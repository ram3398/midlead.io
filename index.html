M U S I N - O N

Musin-on is a music streaming platform built for various blockchain networks that gives users access to a large catalog of music as a freemium streaming. The mission of the Musin-on project is to give everyone freedom to release, distribute and stream any type of audio content. It allows users to listen to the songs in high quality (Original mix) directly from the artist. So the artists are paid from the fans directly without any middleman and also that the peer to peer transactions fully depend on block chain technology and smart contracts.
